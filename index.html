SHOWCASE YOUR VISION

This July holiday, we're inviting all talented photographers from our boys high school to participate in our prestigious photography competition. This is your chance to showcase your unique perspective and creative vision.

Whether you capture the raw beauty of wildlife, the serenity of nature, the vibrance of interesting places and people, or the emotion of special events - we want to see the world through your lens.

The best photographs in each category will be recognized and celebrated. Don't miss this opportunity to leave your mark and build your legacy.
